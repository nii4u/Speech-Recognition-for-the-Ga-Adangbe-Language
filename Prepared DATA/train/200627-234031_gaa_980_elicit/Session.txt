##Ben tsɔɔ mli akɛ ekolɛ dɔkɔdɔkɔ lɛ nɔtsɛ lɛ kɛmiiya jara lɛ nɔ koni eyahɔɔ.
##Dɔkɔdɔkɔ nyɛ kɛ ebii kpaanyɔ miisere blɛoo yɛ kpaakpo ní nɔ efee dioo lɛ toi.
##Nibii etɛ tsara nɔ ba yɛ be kome too mli: 
##Tuŋtɛi babaoo ni amɛtswia lɛ fite Jeep tsɔne ni amɛta mli lɛ fa kome, 
##ni ejwara ashwishwɛi lɛ fɛɛ; 
##David kɛ Rosalía tee amɛhe yɛ mli; 
##asraafonyo lɛ tswa etu lɛ kɛtsɔ hiɛ ashwishwɛ lɛ mli yɛ yitso ŋɛlɛ nɔ.
##Akɛni owula kɛ awula Lin hɔ̃ɔ kpolotooi 
##kɛ dɔkɔdɔkɔi alá yɛ maŋ lɛŋ jara lɛ nɔ hewɔ lɛ, 
##amɛkpɛ amɛyiŋ akɛ amɛbaabi osɔfo lɛ jwɛŋmɔ yɛ he.
##Nakai pɛpɛɛpɛ amɛfee, 
##ni amɛnaa kpɛ amɛhe beni amɛnanemɛi kɛ wuɔi, 
##dɔkɔdɔkɔi, kɛ too kome ke kɛha yookpeemɔ okpɔlɔŋmɛɛ lɛ.
##Efɔ̃ amɛ nine kɛtee etsũ ní ekɛ jwɛi ewo lɛ mli, 
##ni ehe bahia ní amɛkoto shi kɛtsɔ shinaa ní yɔɔ tɛkɛɛ lɛ mli.
##Faai yeɔ ebuaa hu kɛbáa kooloi tamɔ dɔkɔdɔkɔi kɛ 
##Israel maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: 
##Buulu yɔɔ nɛɛ, egbɛ ja yɛ ehiɛ; shi mɔ ni ji nilelɔ lɛ, eboɔ ŋaawoo toi.—Abɛi.
##Kɔɔyɔɔ ni naa wa tswaa be fɛɛ be, 
##ni elooɔ shia kɛshooɔ mɔ hewolo nɔ ni ekɛwoɔ mɔ hiŋmɛiaŋ hu.
##Nakai nɔŋŋ dani abaakpata Sodom kɛ Gomora maji lɛ ahiɛ lɛ,
##Lot shaahii lɛ na lɛ akɛ “etamɔ mɔ ni yeɔ fɛo.”—1 Mose.
##Klɛŋklɛŋ nɔ ni aha ni ji, 
##Gbalashihilɛ ni Aaaha Etsɔ Ekomefeemɔ ni Hiɔ Shi Daa” 
##lɛ kai mɛi ni eba kpee lɛ akɛ 
##yɛ Yehowa hiɛ lɛ gbalashihilɛ jeee gbɛjianɔtoo ni abaanyɛ ashɛ afɔ, 
##taakɛ je lɛŋ bii pii susuɔ lɛ.
##Ákɛ “mɔ fɛɛ mɔ akafo enɔ mli, shi moŋ efo enaanyo nɔ mli.”—Filipibii.
##Shi, kɛtsɔ shwɛde ni haa anaa nii wuji kɛ woji ni akɛ niŋmaa wuji eŋmala lɛ nɔ lɛ,
##minyɛɔ mikɛ ŋmɛlɛtswai enumɔ tsuɔ nii gbi kome 
##ni mikɛkaseɔ nii yɛ be mli ni mikɛ hiŋmɛi kome ni eshwɛ lɛ tsuɔ nii lɛ.
##heloo lɛ sɛɛdii nii lɛ kɛ hiŋmɛii lɛ asɛɛdii nii lɛ kɛ shihilɛ lɛ mli hewoo lɛ, 
##ejɛɛɛ tsɛ lɛ mli, shi moŋ je lɛ mli ejɛ.”
##Kɛji mɔ ko miisumɔ je lɛ, belɛ tsɛ lɛ suɔmɔ lɛ bɛ emli; 
##ejaakɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ je lɛŋ lɛ, 
##heloo lɛ sɛɛdii nii lɛ kɛ hiŋmɛii lɛ asɛɛdii nii lɛ kɛ shihilɛ lɛ mli hewoo lɛ, 
##ejɛɛɛ tsɛ lɛ mli, shi moŋ je lɛ mli ejɛ.
