##Wɔteŋ mɛi babaoo heɔ pɛtro daa; 
##mɛni hewɔ okɛ woji tɛtrɛbii lɛ haaa mɔ ni hɔɔ pɛtro lɛ?
##Tsuifãa sɔŋŋ ha etsi tsɛŋsi kɛ pɛtrol lɛ eshwie mihiɛ trukaa!
##Agbɛnɛ hu, oyɛ hegbɛ akɛ osusuɔ bɔ ni jamɔ aaanyɛ asa Nyɔŋmɔ hiɛ lɛ he, 
##kɛ yiŋtoo akɛ amɛaagbe gbɔmɛi babaoo lɛ.
##Tui ni tswaa tuŋtɛi babaoo shikome tswia tuŋtɛi kɛtee hei ni ataoɔ ni eya lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ; 
##mustard gas ni akɛwuɔ ta lɛ shã, 
##ni epiŋ mɛi, ni eje mɛi komɛi kpá, ni egbe asraafoi akpei abɔ; 
##tawuu tsɔnei miigbɛɛ kɛmiiho yɛ henyɛlɔi ateŋ ni mɔbɔnalɛ bɛ mli, 
##ni tawuu tsɔnei nɛɛ anɔ tui wuji lɛ miigbɛɛ.
##Mishɛɔ mitsɛŋ, ni mijuɔ mihe yɛ nushwiemɔhei ni yɔɔ pɛtro steshɛŋ srɔtoi amli.
##Eji shikpɔŋ lɛ da fe nakai kulɛ,
